Crowfall

Crowfall is an interesting take on the mmorpg genre currently under development. It takes an account progression system and applies it to multi-week rpg campaigns where there is a strong focus on resource gathering and crafting. This is an important feature to me as I find other similar games use resource gathering and crafting as a suppliment to combat, where Crowfall treats them as having equal importance in the war machine.
